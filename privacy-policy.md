## 6. Children's Privacy

### 6.1 Content Audience

Kids Daily Math Challenge produces educational content designed for elementary school students (ages 5-12). However:

- We do not collect any personal information from children
- We do not require account creation to view content
- We do not interact directly with viewers of any age
- All content is hosted on YouTube and subject to YouTube's privacy practices

### 6.2 COPPA Compliance

Our service complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

- No personal information is collected from children under 13
- No registration or account creation is required
- Content is uploaded as "Made for Kids" where appropriate
- We do not use personalized advertising on children's content

### 6.3 Parental Guidance

We recommend that parents and guardians:
- Supervise children's YouTube usage
- Review YouTube's parental control options
- Use YouTube Kids for younger viewers where appropriate

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

### 7.1 Revoking Application Access

If you have authorized our application to access your Google account (applicable only to channel administrators), you can revoke access at any time:

1. Visit **[Google Security Settings](https://security.google.com/settings/security/permissions)**
2. Find "Kids Daily Math Challenge" in the list of connected apps
3. Click **"Remove Access"**

### 7.2 YouTube Viewer Controls

As a YouTube viewer, you have controls provided by Google:
- Manage your Google account privacy settings
- Control your YouTube watch and search history
- Adjust ad personalization preferences

Visit **[Google Account Settings](https://myaccount.google.com/)** to manage your preferences.

### 7.3 Content Concerns

If you have concerns about specific content on our channel:
- Use YouTube's built-in reporting features
- Contact us through YouTube's channel communication features
